## Project Description:

In this project, we address the challenges faced by travelers with disabilities when navigating airport environments. These challenges affect their independence, comfort, and overall travel experience.

The goal of this project is to develop a web-based platform that allows users to request airport assistance services easily and efficiently. Users can select the airport, specify their entrance gate, choose the type of assistance required, and schedule the service in advance.

This system aims to improve accessibility, reduce confusion and delays, and provide a more organized and personalized assistance experience for travelers with disabilities.

## Project Motivation:

Accessibility in airports remains a significant challenge for individuals with disabilities. Many travelers face difficulties in navigating large airport spaces, locating assistance, and receiving support that meets their specific needs.

We chose this problem because it addresses a real-world issue that directly impacts users’ independence and quality of life. Existing solutions are often limited and lack flexibility, as they are typically tied to specific airlines and do not offer personalized support.

Through this project, we aim to enhance accessibility, reduce travel-related stress, and provide a reliable and efficient assistance system that improves the overall airport experience.
